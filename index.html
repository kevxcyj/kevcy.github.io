About me

My name is Kevin Cyrus Jr, an aspiring Full Stack Web Developer. I have many interests when it comes to technology, but I’m mostly interested in understanding how technology works behind the scenes. It's exciting to be pursuing a childhood dream of mine; I’ve always known this was the field I wanted to work in, and I know that I’ll be passionate about any role I land, even though I’m not exactly sure what that will be. A key focus for me right now is my professional growth, especially improving my networking abilities and becoming better at collaborating with peers. I’m working on these skills because I believe that having a strong network will help me pick up new skills and discover new opportunities. I’m excited to find connections with people who share a passion for their work like myself. I’m currently practicing my skills by helping develop a website for a family member. It’s a small starting point that is helping me understand what I'm capable of and what I could improve on. I am excited to see what the future holds, and I believe that I will be a great contributor wherever I get the chance to be.
